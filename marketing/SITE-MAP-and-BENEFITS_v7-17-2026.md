## PART 2 — MEMBER BENEFITS (by audience)

### 🚗 DRIVERS — *"Your own rider base. Keep 100%."*
- **$19.99/mo Private Membership** *(pages say "first month free" — confirm one trial phrase)*
- Your own **"Need a Ride?" ride card** on the site — photo, car, plate, big personal QR, contact, service radius (images included, no extra fees)
- **Personal QR + referral link** → build your **private rider base (client list)** that's yours
- **Keep 100% of fares · 0% commission** · Stripe instant payouts
- **Scheduled rides only** — no on-demand pressure
- **Revenue share** — can't take a ride? share it, still earn a commission
- CBL App **Driver Dashboard**: Clients · Schedule · Message · Rides · Referrals · Driver Tools · Buckee
- **"Verified CBL Driver"** badge · manage everything in CBL Studio
- You stay an **independent contractor** — you choose which rides to accept

### 🧍 RIDERS — *"Your favorite driver, on speed dial."*
- **Free membership** — by invitation, via a driver's/member's QR referral code
- **Limited "Thank You"** for first-time rider members
- Your own **preferred private driver** — schedule rides, message, pay in-app
- **Buckee — AI Travel Concierge** + itinerary planner
- Discover local: **Eats & Drinks, Attractions, Travels/stays** — quick links
- Your own **digital business card + referral QR** — earn on signups you refer
- Directory access: post Rider Requests, browse Classifieds/Shopping/Coupons
- **Private & scheduled** — never surge, never a stranger

### 🍽 RESTAURANTS — *"Get on the map. Powered by CBL."*
- Listed on the **live Eats & Drinks** guide (real ratings/photos) + **Featured** placement
- **Partner tier** (self-serve Stripe checkout): top placement + full site/app access
- **Build your own delivery-driver network** on CBL software *(future — most backend built)*
- **"Powered by OpenTable"** reservation links *(pending OpenTable approval)*
- **Members earn 20% first-year** when a restaurant joins under their code (referral engine)
- Reach local members **and** hotel guests routed by concierges

### 🏨 HOTELS / CONCIERGE — *"Turn your front desk into a local experience engine."*
- **Hotel & Concierge Program** — concierges route guests to local experiences + scheduled rides
- **Hotel Plan $99/mo** (billed annually; **founding rate 50% off year one**)
- **Money-maker + driver magnet** — hotel demand pulls more drivers onto CBL
- Apply on `/concierge` → app partner signup (creates the real records)
